Part 7: Key Learnings

Three insights from 10 years

AI-first was right
We bet on structured data from day one. When the AI wave came, we had data AI could actually use.
Everyone looked the wrong way
Blockchain, smart contracts, that was the hype. LLMs came from the blind spot. Today's hype is probably also partly wrong.
Domain is the moat
AI is commodity. 8 years of legal logic, 300 articles, legal database integration. Technology copies in a day, domain takes years.
Part 8: Conclusion

Back to Lee Sedol

Lee Sedol retired from professional Go in 2019.
He said AI was "impossible to beat".

But here's what people remember:

In game 4, move 78, he did something the AI had never seen.
A move so unexpected that AlphaGo collapsed.

Commentators called it "the most beautiful move in Go history".

AI never found it. 100% human creativity.
